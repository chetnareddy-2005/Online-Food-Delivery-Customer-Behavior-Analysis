# 🍔 Online Food Delivery Customer Behavior Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

## 📊 The Story Behind This Project

While exploring datasets to build my Power BI project, I came across an Online Food Delivery dataset containing information about customers such as age, gender, income level, occupation, family size, and feedback.

Instead of just creating charts, I wanted to analyze customer behavior and understand patterns in food ordering habits.

Some key questions I explored were:

• Which age group orders food the most?
• How does income level influence food orders?
• Which occupation group orders the most?
• What percentage of customers give positive feedback?
• Does family size affect ordering behavior?

Through this project, I analyzed the dataset and created an interactive Power BI dashboard to uncover insights about online food delivery customers.

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

• Microsoft Power BI
• Excel / CSV Dataset
• Data Cleaning & Transformation
• Data Visualization
It also represents my first step into the world of data analytics and storytelling with data.

## 📌 Key Highlights of the Dashboard

• Customer Gender Distribution
• Food Orders by Income Level
• Customer Feedback Distribution
• Food Orders by Occupation
• Food Orders by Family Size
• Customer Orders by Age (Trend Analysis)
• KPI Metrics for quick insights

## 📈 Key Insights

• Most food orders come from customers aged between 22–26.
• Students and employees contribute to the majority of orders.
• Customers with higher income levels tend to order food more frequently.
• A large percentage of customers gave positive feedback.
• Medium family sizes show higher ordering activity.
